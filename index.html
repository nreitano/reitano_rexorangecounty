Who is Rex Orange County?

Rex Orange County, also know as Alexander O'Connor, was born on May 4, 1998. He is an English singer/songwriter from the United Kingdom. His music career started in 2016. His first album was "bcos u will never b free". In 2017, he released his second album "Apricot Princess". He contributed vocals and co-wrote songs with Tyler, the Creator. May 9, 2018, his hit single, "Loving is Easy" broke the charts worldwide. Rex Orange County is an indie pop artist and is pleasing to most of the younger demographic.
